Brief Introduction

What is pilates?

  • 01

    Origin

    Developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century. Based on yoga, martial arts, and ancient Greek and Roman physical regimes.
    6 original principles:
    Concentration, Control, Center, Flow, Precision, Breathing.

  • 02

    What is in our courses?

    We still use much of what Joseph Pilates created originally. Pilates method is a mind and body practice. When done regularly, it can improve flexibility, build strength and develop control and endurance in the body.
